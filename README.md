## 🚀 About Me

I build and share hands-on engineering projects as a hobby. While my professional career is focused on Global Business Development and Strategy, my roots are in **Mechatronics Engineering**. 

This GitHub space is where I reconnect with my engineering soul—prototyping hardware, writing firmware, and exploring the intersection of legacy equipment and modern smart home ecosystems.

---

## 🛠 Featured Projects

### 🏠 Smart Home & Hardware
* **[irriBRANT (ESP32-C6 Irrigation Controller)](https://github.com/Renbrant/ESP32-C6-Irrigation-Controller):** A professional 9-zone smart irrigation controller integrated with Home Assistant via ESPHome.
* **[ESPHome RS232 Controller](https://github.com/Renbrant/ESP32-C6-Serial-Controler):** Bridging legacy AV equipment (like projectors) to modern smart homes using ESP32-C6 and bidirectional serial communication.
* **[Arduino OBS Deck](https://github.com/Renbrant/Arduino_deck):** A DIY hardware controller for OBS Studio using HID emulation and an analog button matrix.

### 🔊 Audio & Precision Tools
* **[Power Supply Monitor](https://github.com/Renbrant/Power-Supply-Monitor-Arduino):** Real-time tracking of voltage and current for audio amplifier testing using ADS1115 and ACS712.
* **[G29 Load Cell Controller](https://github.com/Renbrant/g29-load-cell-brake-controller):** Improving sim-racing precision by converting potentiometer-based pedals to pressure-based load cell systems.

### 📱 Software & Community
* **[Voxyl](https://github.com/Renbrant/voxyl):** A modern social podcast aggregator that merges RSS feeds with social curation and shared playlists.
* **[Camera_CTR](https://github.com/Renbrant/Camera_CTR):** High-speed trigger for lightning photography and time-lapse projects.

## 🎯 Mission

My goal is to inspire Portuguese speakers, especially young Brazilians, to pursue technology. By sharing open-source hardware and detailed tutorials, I want to show that engineering is accessible and deeply rewarding.
